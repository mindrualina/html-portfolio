The best movies according to Alina

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Pearl Harbor

This is one of my favorite movies. A romantic, war movie, portraying World War II, more exactly the Japanese attack on the Pearl Harbor naval base.

The Notebook

Favorite romantic movie, exploring themes of love, memory, and the enduring power of true love.

Shutter Island

A great psychological thriller, with unexpected twists and turns in the plot. Leornardo Dicaprio, playing the main character, is the best.
